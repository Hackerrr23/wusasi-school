We Are Redefining African Education

The Best Bilingual Classes

The main mission of our school is to promote strong values such as Excellence, confidence and integrity for each and every one of ourstudents. Through the International School of Masanjay, students will have the opportunity to receive real bilingual French-Englishdiploma and to travel around the world through our international partners. Our unique program offers everyone the ability to promote aneducation of excellence recognized by all local and international companies. More than just a school, The International School ofMaranjay aims to be the School of reference in Guinea and other countries. All our students are trained to accomplish great projects forour country especially in the medical, technological and scientific fields. The general mission of our school is to be ranked in the top 30private schools of Guinea in the next fifteen years


Employing the latest programselaborated by the Ministry of Education and the best of the American programs, ourestablishment promotesa bilingual pedagogy centered on the needs of each of our students immersed in aninternational community. The teaching method takes in consideration rich, varied experienceswhere each individual’s knowledges and capacities are developed to their highest potential.
