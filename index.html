The International School Of Masanjay

International English-French School

The main mission of our school is to promote strong values such as Excellence, confidence and integrity for each and every one of our students. Through the International School of Masanjay, students will have the opportunity to receive real bilingual French-English diploma and to travel around the world through our international partners. Our unique program offers everyone the ability to promote an education of excellence recognized by all local and international companies.

Our Curriculum Classes

The International School of Masanjay Is Here For All Stages of a Student's Development

  • Pre-School

    The principal aim of each preschool grade at The International School of Masanjay is to guide children in becoming independent, confident learners and to help them acquire knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in elementary school.

  • Lower School

    The personal development of your child, our student, is at the heart of our school. The Lower School focuses its curriculum on a builingual education that is rich in experience and whose pedagogical methods allow each learner to bring out the very best in themselves.

  • Secondary School

    Our Secondary School leads your child, our student, towards a state of responsible self- determination. The child’s personal development is the focal point of our school. All subjects are taught by native, experienced teachers. Our students are taught to succeed at both French and American standardized tests.
